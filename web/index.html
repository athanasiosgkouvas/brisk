Feeless by design

You pay the exact amount. Brisk never takes a cut.

Card networks skim 2–3% off every sale and settle in days. Brisk rides Sui's protocol-level gasless stablecoin transfers, so payments cost $0.00 in gas and there are no fees on regular payments — for the customer or the merchant.

  • Protocol-level gasless USDC — not a relayer trick
  • Sponsored receipts via Enoki, paid by Brisk
  • Money moves even if the receipt leg is skipped

How it works

Built on what changed on Sui in 2026.

Two new primitives make a genuinely better payment experience possible — Brisk puts them together into "tap, pay, done."

Protocol-level gasless transfers

Supported stablecoins move peer-to-peer for $0.00 via Sui's new Address Balances architecture (launched May 2026). The sender doesn't need any SUI for gas — it's built into the protocol, not a relayer trick.

🔐

zkLogin + Passkey + Enoki

Self-custodial accounts from a Google sign-in, biometric signing, and Enoki gas sponsorship — so a wallet feels like a normal app, with no seed phrase and no pop-ups.

🧾

Two-leg payments

Leg one settles the money via native gasless send_funds — the source of truth. Leg two best-effort sponsors record_payment, minting an on-chain Receipt. If leg two is skipped, the money already moved.

📦

Move primitives

A small set of on-chain modules: spending_vault (Save), merchant_registry (identity & tills), and payment_receipt (receipts & records) — composable, auditable, open.

📈

Yield-bearing Save

Save is a cToken/share money market: deposits accrue a compounding exchange rate, principal stays redeemable, and yield is supplied through a blue-chip lender (a mock market on testnet).

🎁

Closed-loop gift cards

Merchants are paid the full sale (minus a 3% fee) the moment a gift card is bought. Recipients later redeem credit toward purchases — buyable, shareable, claimable and re-giftable, all gasless.
